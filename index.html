About Our Startup

Dream Tech is a software development company founded by four passionate university students from Tanzania. We're driven by a desire to empower fellow students and local entrepreneurs with innovative and affordable software solutions.


Our team brings together talented developers, UI/UX designers, sales personnel and project managers, fueled by the knowledge gained from our side hustles, online courses and our entrepreneurship program.



Our team may be young, but our education provides a strong foundation for success. The knowledge gained from our entrepreneurship program equips us with the skills needed to navigate the business landscape and deliver exceptional service. We are confident in Dream Tech's ability to become a leading provider of software solutions for Tanzania's growing entrepreneurial ecosystem.



The market potential for affordable software solutions in Tanzania is significant. According to recent studies, the number of startups and small businesses in Tanzania is on the rise, with many driven by young entrepreneurs. The continued growth in internet penetration and smartphone usage further supports the expansion of the software market.
